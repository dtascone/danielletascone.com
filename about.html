About

Hi!  I am Danielle, and I am a hair artist.  I have always had high hopes of making people feel better about themselves, and I have found a way of doing so through hair.  Making others happy makes me happy.

Besides hair I love spending time with my family and friends, especially when food is involved!  When I'm taking it easy, you can find me on my couch with my dog, shopping, or if weather permits, snowboarding.  I have always been know to be down for a weekend roadtrip, or any trip for that matter.  Going to new places, experiencing different cultures, and meeting new people intrigues me.

Traveling is not only fun but it inspires me!  From the lines of buildings and trees, to the lines of different peoples hair; new places and faces provide me with excitement and new ideas.  I like to keep my mind fresh so I can 'freshen up' my clients.  Thank you for taking the time to get to know me, I wish you a safe journey, and I hope to get to know you soon!
